Story

We are in the planet Croquetania, where live and a flurry advanced race of dogs which loves tech, the space and dog kibbles.


Here lives our hero Toddy who is a astronaut pilot, suddenly he detected an entire fleet of Space Flying Cats with the objective to delete all kind of dog food (they only like fish and tuna).


Toddy without a second thought, decided to get his ship and save the dog kibble by using the last high tech defend system against cats, water and bleach.
